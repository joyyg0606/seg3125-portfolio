About Me

Hi, my name is Joy Yeung. I am a Software Engineering student at the University of Ottawa. I am interested in technology, design, and building websites that are simple and easy to use.

This portfolio will be used to organize my design work in SEG3125. As the semester goes on, I will add more projects and improve the design based on what I learn in class.

How I Work

Learning First

I am currently learning UI and UX design principles in SEG3125, including visual communication, layout, color, typography, and user-friendly design.

Simple Design

My goal is to make interfaces that are clean, organized, and easy to understand. I try to avoid clutter and focus on clear sections.

Improving Over Time

This portfolio is a first version. I plan to keep improving it as I learn more design concepts and complete more assignments.
